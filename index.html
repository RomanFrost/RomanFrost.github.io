My Story

Competing at the Highest Level —
Now Preparing for What's Next

My name is Roman Frost. I am 26 years old, originally from Germany, and I am currently living in Salt Lake City, Utah. Skiing has shaped my entire life, and it is also the reason I ended up here.

I spent over a decade competing at the national and international level — leaving education aside to focus fully on my sport. Now is the time for me to prepare for what's coming next. I have already taken the first step, earning my Bachelor's degree while competing at Westminster University. One more push is all it takes to complete my Master's at the University of Utah and set myself up for the future.
